## Claiming Leads

### How Lead Distribution Works

1. When a new lead arrives, the system identifies eligible agents based on:
   - **Language match** - You must speak the lead's language
   - **Availability** - You must be marked as accepting new leads
   - **Capacity** - You must not be at your maximum lead limit

2. Eligible agents receive notifications via:
   - In-app notification (bell icon)
   - Email notification
   - Optional: Slack notification

3. You have **15 minutes** to claim the lead (claim window)

### Claiming a Lead

1. Click the notification or find the lead in "New Leads" section
2. Review the lead information:
   - Name, phone, email
   - Language
   - Budget and timeframe
   - Property preferences
3. Click **"Claim This Lead"**
4. The lead is now assigned to you

**Important:** 
- First click wins - if another agent clicks simultaneously, only one will succeed
- If no one claims within 15 minutes, the lead goes to the admin for manual assignment

### What Happens After Claiming

- The lead appears in your "My Leads" list
- Your lead count increases
- Other agents no longer see this lead
- You should contact the lead as soon as possible (SLA target: within 4 hours)

## Logging Activities

### Log a Call

After every phone call, log it:

1. Click **"Log Call"** button on the lead page
2. The call timer starts automatically (or enter duration manually)
3. Select the **outcome**:
   - **Answered** - Had a conversation
   - **No Answer** - Phone rang, no pickup
   - **Busy** - Line was busy
   - **Voicemail** - Left a message
   - **Wrong Number** - Incorrect contact info
   - **Not Interested** - Lead declined

4. For "Answered" calls, also select:
   - **Interest Level**: Very Interested, Interested, Neutral, Not Interested
   
5. Add detailed **notes** about the conversation
6. Optionally **schedule a callback**
7. Click **"Save Activity"**

**Tips for good notes:**
- What did you discuss?
- What are their requirements?
- Any objections or concerns?
- What's the next step?

### WhatsApp Follow-Up

If a call goes unanswered:

1. Click **"Send WhatsApp"**
2. Select a message template:
   - Standard Follow-up
   - Missed Call
   - Property Information
   - Viewing Invitation
   - Thank You
   - Custom Message
3. The message will have {tokens} replaced with actual names
4. Click **"Open WhatsApp"** - it will open wa.me with the message
5. Confirm you sent the message to log the activity

### Log an Email

1. Click **"Log Email"**
2. Enter the email subject
3. Add notes about the content
4. Optionally schedule a follow-up
5. Click **"Save Activity"**

### Add a Note

For internal observations:

1. Click **"Add Note"**
2. Enter your note
3. Select type: **Private** (only you) or **Shared** (visible to admin)
4. Optionally **pin** important notes
5. Click **"Save Note"**

### Managing Reminders

For each reminder, you can:

#### Complete
- Click when you've done the callback/task
- Removes from pending list

#### Snooze
- Postpone for: 30 min, 1 hour, 4 hours
- Or set a custom time

#### Reschedule
- Change to a completely new date/time

#### Delete
- Remove the reminder entirely

1. From a lead page, click **"Set Reminder"**
2. Quick options:
   - Tomorrow 10:00 AM

3. Or pick a custom date and time
4. Select reminder type: Callback, Follow-up, Viewing, Meeting
5. Add a note about what to discuss
6. Click **"Create Reminder"**

## Best Practices

### Response Time

- **Goal:** Contact new leads within 4 hours
- **Why:** Fast response dramatically increases conversion
- **SLA Warning:** If no contact within 24 hours, admin is alerted

### Activity Logging

- Log **every** interaction (calls, emails, WhatsApp)
- Add detailed notes - future you will thank you
- Update lead status after each interaction
- Schedule follow-ups immediately after calls

### Lead Qualification

High-quality leads typically have:
- Specific location preferences
- Clear budget range
- Defined timeframe (within 1 year)

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't see any leads"

- Check if you're marked as "Accepts New Leads" (ask admin)
- Verify your language settings
- Check if you're at maximum lead capacity

### "Claim button didn't work"

- Another agent may have claimed first
- The claim window may have expired
- Try refreshing the page

### "My reminder didn't show"

- Check the date/time was set correctly
- Look in different calendar views
- Verify the reminder was saved (check history)

### "I'm not receiving email notifications"

- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify your email address is correct
- Contact admin to verify settings
